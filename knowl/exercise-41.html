Exercise 4.

Determine whether each molecular statement below is true or false, or whether it is impossible to determine. Assume you do not know what my favorite number is (but you do know which numbers are prime).
  1. If 19 is not prime, then 19 is my favorite number.
  2. If 4 is prime, then \(2\cdot4\) is prime
  3. If 4 is my favorite number, then 4 is prime.
  4. 4 is my favorite number or 4 is not prime.
  5. If 4 is my favorite number, then 19 is prime
  6. 4 is my favorite number or 19 is not my favorite number.
