Solution 1.1.5.1.

The main thing to realize is that we do not know the colors of these two shapes, but we do know that we are in one of three cases: We could have a purple triangle and orange diamond. We could have a triangle that was not purple but a orange diamond. Or we could have a triangle that was not purple and a diamond that was not orange. The case in which the triangle is purple but the diamond is not orange cannot occur, as that would make the statement false.
